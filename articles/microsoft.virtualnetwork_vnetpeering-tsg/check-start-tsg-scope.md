# Check if this TSG applies to the customer scenario

Verify the customer issue is a match for this ***Virtual Network Peering*** troubleshooting guide. Customers often misunderstand or misapply the term 'peering'. They will often apply it to VPN or ExpressRoute issues.

## **How to verify customer issue is in scope for this TSG**

1. Check the customer verbatim to determine if it is clear they are having issues with Vnet Peering
2. Use the details in the customer verbatim and review 'Network Topology' in Azure Support Center > Tools to validate customer issue maybe related to VNet peering
3. Contact the customer to scope the issue and determine if customer issue is related to VNet peering
