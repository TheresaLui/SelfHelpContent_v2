# Get more information and support on partner training tools

Get more information and support on partner training tools

## **Recommended Steps**

 - Sign in to [Partner University](https://partneruniversity.microsoft.com/?whr=default) with the personal Microsoft Account you used to associate to your company's MPN ID account. 
 
 - If you encounter problems logging in, please go [here](https://partner.microsoft.com/support/v2) and type login issue - this will not require a sign in to submit a ticket
 
 - For Dynamics Learning portal go [here](https://mbs.microsoft.com/PartnerSource/northamerica/Help/Help/ContactUs)
