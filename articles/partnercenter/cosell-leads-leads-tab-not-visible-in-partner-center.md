# Cosell Leads Leads tab not visible in Partner Center

## **Recommended Steps**

1. Clear your browser cookies & cache and refresh the page
2. Verify you have the role of *‘Referrals admin’*. You can view your assigned role in *‘Your Partner Center profile (on the top right corner) > My profile > Account > Partner Center permissions -> ‘Click here to view permissions’*.

**Note**: Please include your MPN ID, a screenshot of the error & the [HTTP archive file](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/azure-portal/capture-browser-trace) when filing a support ticket.
