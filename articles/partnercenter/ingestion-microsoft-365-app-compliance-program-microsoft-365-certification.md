# Ingestion Microsoft 365 App Compliance program Microsoft 365 Certification

Pursuing a certification is an impactful way of showing enterprise organizations your commitment to upholding security best practices, and the first step toward building a trusted ecosystem of apps ready for enterprise consumption.

## **Recommended Steps**

* Microsoft will respond with a request for evidence, outlining which controls will be in-scope for your assessment. You will have 60 days from this point to submit all required evidence.

* Submit all evidence to Microsoft within the 60-day period.

* Expect an email from a Microsoft contact with clarifying questions, or supplemental evidence requests to assist in completing your assessment.
