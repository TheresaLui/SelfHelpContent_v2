# Partner Center Incentives: Claims approval and payment deadlines

## **Recommended Steps**

**When will my co-op claim will be approved?**
* Co-op claims are reviewed within **two business days** after they have been submitted. Once they are deemed valid, they will be set to Approved status.  

Below are the possible statuses for co-op claims:
* Submitted
* Under review (Microsoft will complete its review within two business days of the claim being submitted. If your status is Under review for longer than two days, contact Support.)
* Action required
* Disputed
* Editing 
* Approved
* Rejected

**When will I get the payment for the approved co-op claim?**
* Payments will be made within **45 business days** of the approval date. 
* If the program needs a **tax invoice**, payments will be made within **30 days after tax invoice is approved**.
