# Problems renewing my subscription

## **Recommended Steps**

By default, active subscriptions are set to automatically renew when the subscription period expires. For subscriptions to commercial marketplace products, you can optionally choose not to [automatically renew the subscription](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/create-a-new-subscription#choose-whether-to-automatically-renew-a-commercial-marketplace-subscription).
Subscriptions which auto-renew retain the same subscription start date.

For help renewing your MPN Internal Use License Rights subscription, create a service request [here](https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard/support/csp/servicerequests/create?stage=2&topicid=d1cd8813-7325-bafd-b4af-242c101b60ef).

For help renewing a web-direct or VL subscription, [create a service request on behalf of your customer](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/report-problems-on-behalf-of-a-customer) via the appropriate product portal.
