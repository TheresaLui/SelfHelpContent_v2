# Invoices and taxes in Partner Center

Due to unforeseen issues we will not be able to offer credit card payment option for Japan, South Korea and Taiwan until further notice. You will notice that you can still make your purchase seamlessly via Wire Transfer. Please contact your financial institution for more information on how to execute a wire transfer.

## **Recommended Steps**

- You must be a Global administrator or MPN Partner administrator to perform this procedure
- Sign into [Partner Center](https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard/home) with your work account, and select **MPN** on the left menu
- In the left navigation menu under MPN, select **Membership offers** to see the offers available for purchase, as well as the invoices available for the current year of enrollment. To view invoices for previous years, select **Expired**.
- Select the invoice you want, and then click on **Download invoice**
- Please know that Invoices are generally available ten days after payment is complete
- For additional information, contact your Regional Service Center
