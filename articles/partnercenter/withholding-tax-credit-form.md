# Make sure you are credited for withholding tax

You need to include your withholding tax credit form and a digital copy of your Withholding Tax Certificate with the support request.


## **Recommended Steps**

- Ensure the form includes your name and address, that Microsoft Corporation or Microsoft is completely spelled out (the terms MSFT, MS Corp or other variations are not acceptable)
 and the form is signed and dated accurately. Here are the tax credit forms: For [Germany](https://query.prod.cms.rt.microsoft.com/cms/api/am/binary/RE305Lo) 
 and [All other countries](https://query.prod.cms.rt.microsoft.com/cms/api/am/binary/RE30311).

- Accurately record the proper withholding tax amount % based on your local tax authority information.

- Be sure the total amount on the tax credit form matches the totals from the invoices you are submitting. 

- Once you this information ready, create the service request and be sure to attache the withholding tax credit form and your Withholding Tax Certificate.
