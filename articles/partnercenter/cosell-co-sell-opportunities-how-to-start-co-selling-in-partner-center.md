# Cosell Co-sell Opportunities How to start Co-selling in Partner Center

## **Recommended Steps**

1. Create your offer in the [Commercial Marketplace hub of Partner Center](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/marketplace/partner-center-portal/commercial-marketplace-overview)
2. Complete the [offer ingestion process](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/marketplace/partner-center-portal/commercial-marketplace-co-sell) to make your offer Co-sell ready
3. Ensure you have the *‘Referrals admin’* role so you can create & manage Co-sell opportunities in Partner Center. You can view your assigned role in your *Partner Center profile (on the top right corner) > My profile > Account > Partner Center permissions -> 'Click here to view permissions'.*

**Note**: If you still see a *‘Get Co-sell ready’* message in Partner Center, please include your MPN ID & your solution ID when filing a support ticket.
