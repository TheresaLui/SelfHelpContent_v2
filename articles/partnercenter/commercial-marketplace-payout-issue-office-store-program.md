# Commercial Marketplace - Payout issue Office store program

All paid add-ins in AppSource are hidden and are no longer available to new customers. Existing paid add-in subscriptions have been cancelled other than a small set of SharePoint add-in subscription that are scheduled to be cancelled on September 30, 2020.  You will continue to be paid for any existing active subscriptions until they are cancelled.
