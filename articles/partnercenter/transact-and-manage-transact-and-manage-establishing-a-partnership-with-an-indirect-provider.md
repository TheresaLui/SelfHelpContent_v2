# Transact and Manage Transact and Manage Establishing a partnership with an Indirect provider

## **Recommended Steps**

Partner transacting in the Indirect channel (purchasing on behalf of customers through an Indirect Provider) should establish a partnership relationship in Partner center in order to be able to see and jointly manage customers.

The steps for Indirect providers to establish a partnership with an Indirect reseller are detailed [here](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/indirect-provider-tasks-in-partner-center#connect-with-indirect-resellers).

The step for Indirect resellers to accept a partnership are detailed [here](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/indirect-reseller-tasks-in-partner-center#connect-with-an-indirect-provider).

**Note**: in order to accept a partnership request from an Indirect Provider, the Indirect reseller must:

1. have first enrolled as an Indirect reseller and accepted the MPA.  Please refer to [Enrolling as an Indirect reseller support topic](https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard/support/csp/servicerequests/create?stage=2&topicid=7edb156d-e775-c0f0-2943-1eac0738046a) for detailed steps and requirements.
2. be in the same [CSP country and region](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/indirect-reseller-tasks-in-partner-center#your-csp-country-and-the-csp-regions-where-you-do-business) as the Indirect provider

For more information on supported partner relationships in partner center please refer [here](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/csp-supported-partner-relationships).
