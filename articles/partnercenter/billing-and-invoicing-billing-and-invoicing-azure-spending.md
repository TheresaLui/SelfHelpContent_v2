# Billing and Invoicing - Billing and Invoicing - Azure spending

Azure monthly spending email notification feature in Partner Center to allow multiple Admin Agents to receive notifications. This allows for scale across your organization to better assist your customers in managing their spending budget for this important part of their business. This will replace the existing notification setting on the Azure spending page. To receive Azure spending notices, all Admin Agents must configure their email in the [My preferences](https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard/engagement/preference) section of Partner Center.

## **Recommended Steps**

To configure the new Azure spending email notification, follow these steps:
* Sign in to Partner Center.
* Select **My preferences** from **Settings**.
* Set your preferred email address to get the notification.
* Select **CSP** from the **Notification preferences** section, and check **Azure spending** option.
* Click **Save preferences**.
