# Business Central Development support

Please file tickets in this category only if your issue regards the Dynamics 365 Business Central product and requires technical support from the support and development teams

## **Recommended Steps**

* If your issue relates to **publishing and validating your app for publication on AppSource** please select a different category under Commercial Marketplace in this form for the ticket to be routed to the correct team
* If your issue pertains to **a single Dynamics 365 Business Central customer**, please file the support ticket on the [Power Platform Admin Center](https://admin.powerplatform.microsoft.com/) as the Delegated Admin for it to be routed to the correct team
* If your issue is a **bug in the Dynamics 365 Business Central product**, please proceed and provide detailed reproduction steps and explain the expected behavior

* [Business Central resources for Help and Support](https://docs.microsoft.com/dynamics365/business-central/dev-itpro/help-and-support)
