# Available licenses vs. sold licenses

## **Recommended Steps**

* **Add or remove user licenses for a customer**
- Sign into the **Partner Center dashboard**
- From the Partner Center menu, select **Customers**, then choose a customer from the list
- In the customer menu, select **Users and licenses**
- Choose one or more users from the list. If, for example, the customer just purchased new licenses and you wanted to assign them to people who don't have them yet, you can use the **Filter users by** option to find the right group
- Select **Manage licenses**. Make your changes, then **Save**

* [Assign licenses to a user](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/assign-licenses-to-users)

* [Assign or revoke licenses to multiple users](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/bulk-license-provisioning-for-multiple-users)
