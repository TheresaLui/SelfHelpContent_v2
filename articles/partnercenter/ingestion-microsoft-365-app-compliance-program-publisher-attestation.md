# Ingestion Microsoft 365 App Compliance program Publisher Attestation

Publisher Attestation is a voluntary program where you complete a self-assessment of your app's security, data handling, and compliance practices. The information you provide will be presented to your customers so they can better evaluate your app before enabling it for their organization.

## **Recommended Steps**

* Attestation is managed in Partner Center by navigating to Office Store select Overview then select an app and another left navigation will pop up select App Compliance. This will start the Publisher Attestation process.
