# <-- SaaS migration deflection -->

If you are experiencing any issues with your offer migrating from Cloud Partner Portal to Partner Center, try the steps outlined below.

## **Recommended Steps**
1. Look for the yellow banner in the Cloud Partner Portal on your Publisher Profile page indicating the state of your migration
2. Confirm you have taken the appropriate follow up action to [migrate your account to Partner Center](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/marketplace/partner-center-portal/account-migration-from-cpp-to-pc#account-migration-process)
3. If still encountering access issues, verify you are inspecting the correct publisher ID in Cloud Partner Portal if you happen to have multiple IDs. Submit a support ticket if access cannot be resolved through the provided guidance.
