# How to become an Indirect Reseller

## **Recommended Steps**

Before you proceed, we **highly recommend** you read  [MPA FAQ](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/mpa-indirect-provider-faq) to understand your MPN and CSP account structure in Partner Center and other common questions to help you with this onboarding. 

1. [Download](https://assetsprod.microsoft.com/mpn/migrate-pmc-pc-mpa-guide.pptx) the step-by-step guide to migrate your PMC account to PC. It also includes troubleshooting steps.
2. To verify your MPN account is in Partner Center, [Click here](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/microsoft-partner-agreement#verify-your-mpn-id-is-active-and-migrated-to-partner-center)
3. If you donâ€™t have an MPN account or ID, you need to join the Microsoft Partner Network program first, [Click here](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/mpn-create-a-partner-center-account)
4. To verify you are already enrolled as CSP Indirect Reseller in Partner Center, [Click here](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/microsoft-partner-agreement#confirm-you-are-enrolled-as-a-csp-indirect-reseller)
5. For help on which MPN ID to associate while enrolling into CSP Indirect Reseller program or create a new MPN location(MPN ID), [Click here](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/manage-locations#view-your-mpn-locations)
6. To accept Microsoft Partner Agreement in Partner Center, login to [Partner Center](https://partner.microsoft.com/pcv/dashboard/overview) using Global Admin credentials. You can confirm if the Microsoft Partner Agreement has already been signed from [Agreement](https://partner.microsoft.com/dashboard/account/v3/settings/agreements) or the banner notification on the CSP Overview page. 
7. To create a new CSP Indirect reseller account in another CSP region, [Click here](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/manage-locations#prerequisites-in-order-to-add-a-new-account-for-a-csp-business)
8. If you already have a CSP Indirect Reseller account but having trouble with the account verification step, [Click here](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/verification-responses).
9. To connect with an Indirect Provider in Partner Center, [Click here](https://docs.microsoft.com/partner-center/indirect-reseller-tasks-in-partner-center#connect-with-an-indirect-provider)
