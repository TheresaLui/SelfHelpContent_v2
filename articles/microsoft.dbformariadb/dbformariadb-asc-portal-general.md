* Clear your browser cache
* Refresh your browser
* If above steps don't work, you can always unblock yourself by using [Azure CLI](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/cli/azure/MariaDB?view=azure-cli-latest) to manage your Azure Database for MariaDB servers.
