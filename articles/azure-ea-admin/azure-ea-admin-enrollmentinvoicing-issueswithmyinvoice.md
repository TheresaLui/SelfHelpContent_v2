# Issues with my invoice

If you think you have an incorrect overage invoice, see [Understand your Azure Enterprise Agreement bill](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/billing/billing-understand-your-bill-ea) to help you reconcile your Azure usage charges.

You can also find more information under [view price sheet information](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/billing/billing-ea-portal-enrollment-invoices#view-price-sheet-information) and  [request detailed usage information to be able to reconcile your charges on your invoice](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/billing/billing-ea-portal-enrollment-invoices#request-detailed-usage-information).
