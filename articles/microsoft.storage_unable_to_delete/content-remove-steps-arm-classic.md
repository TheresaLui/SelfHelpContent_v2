## **Recommended Steps**

1. Enter [**Azure Support Center (ASC)**](https://azuresupportcenter.msftcloudes.com/ticketdetails) and select the case being investigated.
2. Navigate to **Resource Explorer** and search for the affected **Storage Account** resource.
3. Select the **Disks/Images** tab from the Storage Account section.
4. Review the contents listed under **Disks**, **OS Images** and **VM Images**. **Note**: Both ARM and Classic resources are displayed here regardless on the Storage Account deployment model.
5. Pay attention to the amount of the above objects as you'll need it for the next step.
