# How to remove the subscription spending limit

1. The spending limit feature enabled can protect the customer from charges when the monthly credits are exhausted, removing it means any usage after exhaustion of credits will be billed to the PI.
2. To remove the spending limit, the customer must do it from the Azure Portal.
3. ASMS cannot remove the spending limit on behalf of the customer.
4. Account Admin must perform this steps
