# Enable subscription due to CapReached

<!--issueDescription-->

1. Sign in to the Azure Portal.
2. Search for subscription in the portal search bar.
3. Select the subscription that was created when you signed up for Azure free account.
4. In the subscription overview, click Upgrade subscription button in the command bar. If you don't see the upgrade subscription button, click on the upgrade banner at the top of the page.
5. If you don't have a payment method for your account, you'll be prompted to add one.
6. You might need to enter a phone number to verify your identity.
7. Type a name for your subscription.
8. Choose a support plan (if needed) for your subscription. To learn more about support plans, see Azure support plans.
9. Click Upgrade.

<!--/issueDescription-->
