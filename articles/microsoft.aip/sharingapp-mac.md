### RMS Sharing App for Mac

RMS sharing app enables you to view protected files that others have shared with you (.ppdf, .ptxt, .pxml, .pjpg, .pjpeg, .ppng, .ptiff, .pgif, .pbmp, and .pfile file extensions).
