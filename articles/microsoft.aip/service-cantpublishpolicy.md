# Azure Information service - can't publish policy

## Recommended troubleshooting steps

1. Review the documentation for [configuring the Azure Information Protection policy](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/information-protection/deploy-use/configure-policy).
2. After a policy is changed and you select **Save**, the policy is published.
3. If you experience any error during this process, take a screenshot of the error and attach it to the support ticket, adding the timestamp when the error  occurred.
