# DS4DC1 Reduce parallel of stored procedure

<br>
This **Solution 4** suggests reducing parallel of stored procedure to avoid rate limiting/throttling(429) first. 
<br>If the solution could not solve the problem, it suggests in turn Solution 8 to increase retries/wait time or RU Limit or reduce required RU consumption.
