### Check if enabling Autoscale saves RU consumption
Calculate average normalized utilization across all hours with metric “Normalized RU Utilization”.
<br>If you see that your average utilization is less than 66%, consider enabling autoscale on your database or container. In contrast, if the average utilization is greater than 66%, it's recommended to remain on standard (manual) provisioned throughput.
