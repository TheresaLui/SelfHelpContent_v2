# TS4 SC5 Detected throttling due to Avg RPS is higher than what can be served

<br>
#### **Detected rate limiting/throttling(429) due to Avg RPS is higher than what can be served.**

<br> To continue troubleshooting this issue, please select and follow **Solution 8**
 to suggest the customer trying the following 3 workaround.

<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;**Solution 8 Increase retries/wait time or RU Limit or reduce required RU consumption**

* Increase retries/wait timer
* Increase RU Limit
* Reduce required RU consumption
