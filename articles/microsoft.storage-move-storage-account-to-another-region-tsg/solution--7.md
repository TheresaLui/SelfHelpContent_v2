# Account cannot be moved to the target region

<!--issueDescription-->

We are unable to detach disks which exists in the source storage account. Please refer/follow compute TSG to stop and detach disks [Windows](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/virtual-machines/windows/detach-disk) / [Linux](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/virtual-machines/linux/detach-disk) from VM. Please come back and continue this TSG once detached.
