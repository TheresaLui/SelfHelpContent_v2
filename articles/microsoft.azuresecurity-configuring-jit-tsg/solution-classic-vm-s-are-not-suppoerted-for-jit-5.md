# Classic VMs are not suppoerted for JIT

<!--issueDescription-->

Dear Customer, <br>
Classic VM's are not suppoerted for Security Center just-in-time access.<br>
Please refer to Docs: <br>
[Secure your management ports with just-in-time access](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/security-center/security-center-just-in-time?tabs=jit-config-asc%2Cjit-request-asc).<br>
<br>
Please consider [converting your classic VMs to ARM](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/virtual-machines/migration-classic-resource-manager-overview)<br>
<br>
Best Regards,
