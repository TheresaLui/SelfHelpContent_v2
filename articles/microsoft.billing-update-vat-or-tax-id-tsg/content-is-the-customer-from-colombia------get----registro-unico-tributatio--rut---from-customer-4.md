# Is the customer from Colombia? - Get Registro Unico Tributatio (RUT) from customer

Now we need the customer to provide the correct form called Registro Unico Tributation "RUT" and make sure it is completed correctly

# Recommended Steps

1. Call the customer and have them email you the form and copy the case mail.
2. Ensure it is filled out completely and it is attached to the SR.
3. Locate the Numero de Identification Tributaria (NIT)
4. Validate NIT using [this link](https://muisca.dian.gov.co/WebRutMuisca/DefConsultaEstadoRUT.faces)
4. Customer's name and address.
5. Microsoft Corporation or Microsoft is completely spelled out (the terms MSFT, MS Corp or other variations are not acceptable).
6. Form is signed and dated.
