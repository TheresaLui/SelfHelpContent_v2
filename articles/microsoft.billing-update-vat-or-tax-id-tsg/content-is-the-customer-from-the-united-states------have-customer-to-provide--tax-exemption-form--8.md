# Is the customer from The United States? 

1. Have customer to provide "Tax Exemption Form"
2. Now we need the customer to provide the correct form called Tax Exemption Form and make sure it is completed correctly
3. Call the customer and have them email you the form and copy the case mail.
4. Make sure all of the following information is correctly added

1. Customer's name and address.
2. Seller (MS) Name/Address, Microsoft Corporation or Microsoft is completely spelled out (the terms MSFT, MS Corp or other variations are not acceptable).
3. Customer (Tax Payer) Full legal Name
4. Signature and/or effective date of exemption
5. State(s) the certificate is applicable to
5. Customer (Tax payer) Number
6. Reason for exemption
7. Type of Property/Service being purchased
8. If certificate is applicable for all current and future transactions or just the current transaction
9. Form is signed and dated.
