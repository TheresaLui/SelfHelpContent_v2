# Configure BGP, routing, forced tunneling 

## Configure BGP, routing, forced tunneling 

Border Gateway Protocol (BCP) enables the Azure VPN Gateways and your on-premises VPN devices (called *BGP peers* or *neighbors*) to exchange routes that inform both gateways on the availability and reachability for those prefixes to go through the gateways or routers involved. BGP can also enable transit routing among multiple networks. Forced tunneling lets you redirect all Internet-bound traffic back to your on-premises location through a Site-to-Site VPN tunnel for inspection and auditing.
