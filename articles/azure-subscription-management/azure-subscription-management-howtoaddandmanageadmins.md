# how to add and manage admins

## **Recommended steps**
There are three kinds of administrator roles in Azure- Account Administrator (authorized to manage core billing & subscription management tasks), Service Admin (authorized to manage services) and Co-Admin (same privileges as Service Admin).

1. Steps to add Co-Adminstrator for a subscription<br>
[How to add an Co-admin?](https://azure.microsoft.com/documentation/articles/billing-add-change-azure-subscription-administrator/#how-to-add-an-admin-for-a-subscription)
2. Steps to change 'Service Adminstrator' for a subscription<br>
[How to change Service Administrator?](https://azure.microsoft.com/documentation/articles/billing-add-change-azure-subscription-administrator/#how-to-change-service-administrator-for-a-subscription)
3. Steps to change 'Account Adminstrator' for a subscription<br>
[How to change the Account Administrator?](https://azure.microsoft.com/documentation/articles/billing-add-change-azure-subscription-administrator/#how-to-change-the-account-administrator)
4. To determine the 'Account Administrator' of your subscription - click on 'Subscriptions' on the left pane in Azure portal, select your subscription, click 'Settings', select 'Properties' and scroll down on the properties blade to view the Account Admin's email address.
