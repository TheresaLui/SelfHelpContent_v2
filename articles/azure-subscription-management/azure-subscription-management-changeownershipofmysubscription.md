# change ownership of my subscription

## **Recommended steps**
1. The Account Admin can transfer ownership to another user through the Azure Account Center.<br>
[Azure Account Center](https://account.windowsazure.com/Subscriptions)
2. To determine the 'Account Administrator' of your subscription - click on 'Subscriptions' on the left pane in Azure portal, select your subscription, click 'Settings', select 'Properties' and scroll down on the properties blade to view the Account Admin's email address.
3. Learn more about  Azure Administrator roles.<br>
[Azure Administrator roles - types, permissions and limits](https://azure.microsoft.com/documentation/articles/billing-add-change-azure-subscription-administrator/)
