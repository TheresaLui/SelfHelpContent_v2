# Error UserErrorRestoreOperationInProgress

<!--issueDescription-->
We have identified that your restore operation failed because another restore operation is currently in progress. You cannot trigger another restore until the previous job completes.
<!--/issueDescription-->

## **Recommended Step**
To resolve this issue wait for the previous restore job to complete and retry the operation.
