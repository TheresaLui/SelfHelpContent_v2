# ADLS Gen1 - recover deleted account
 

We no longer provide support for recovering a deleted ADLS Gen1 account. As part of our <a href="https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/trust-center/privacy">data privacy guarantee</a>, we ensure that data deleted by our customers is eventually overwritten. 

## **Recommended steps**


Follow our <a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/data-lake-store/data-lake-store-disaster-recovery-guidance">best practices for protecting your data</a> to ensure that accidentally deleted content can be recovered in the future.
