# Configure ExpressRoute Circuit

## Configure ExpressRoute Circuit

Use the ExpressRoute circuit to extend your on-premises networks into the Microsoft cloud over a private connection facilitated by a connectivity provider. With ExpressRoute, you can establish connections to Microsoft cloud services, such as Microsoft Azure, Office 365, and Dynamics 365.

## **Resources**

* Create and modify an ExpressRoute circuit using the [Azure portal](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/expressroute/expressroute-howto-circuit-portal-resource-manager), [PowerShell](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/expressroute/expressroute-howto-circuit-arm), or [CLI](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/expressroute/howto-circuit-cli)
