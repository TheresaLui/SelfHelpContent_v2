# This is the title of the article. It is not displayed in the portal but is a required part of every article.

Most users are able to resolve issues with sensitivity labels by using the steps below.

## **Recommended Steps**

In Purview, classifications are similar to subject tags, and are used to mark and identify content of a specific type that's found within your data estate during scanning.
Sensitivity labels are used to identify classification type categories within your organizational data, as well as the group the policies that you want to apply to each category.

Purview uses the same sensitive information types as Microsoft 365, which lets you to stretch your existing security policies and protection across your entire content and data estate.
