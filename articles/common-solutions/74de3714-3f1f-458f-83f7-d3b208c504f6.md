# Issues related to Publisher Verification for developers

Most users are able to resolve their issues with Publisher Verification issue using the steps below.

## **Recommended Steps**

1. Review the [prerequisites and other requirements](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/active-directory/develop/publisher-verification-overview#requirements) to ensure they are all met
2. Review the instructions to [mark an app as publisher verified](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/active-directory/develop/mark-app-as-publisher-verified) and ensure all steps have been performed successfully
3. Review the list of [common issues](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/active-directory/develop/troubleshoot-publisher-verification#common-issues) and follow the recommended steps if you have an issue on the list
4. Reproduce the request using [Graph Explorer](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/active-directory/develop/troubleshoot-publisher-verification#making-microsoft-graph-api-calls) to gather additional info and rule out any issues in the UI

## **Recommended Documents**

* [Publisher verification - overview](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/active-directory/develop/publisher-verification-overview)
* [Mark your app as publisher verified](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/active-directory/develop/mark-app-as-publisher-verified)
* [Troubleshoot publisher verification](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/active-directory/develop/troubleshoot-publisher-verification)
