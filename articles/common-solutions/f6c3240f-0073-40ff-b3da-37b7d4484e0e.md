# Passwordless method registration and management issues

Most users are able to resolve their Passwordless registration and management issue using the steps below.

## **Recommended Steps**

Users register themselves for the passwordless authentication method of Azure AD. See [User registration and management of Microsoft Authenticator](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/active-directory/authentication/howto-authentication-passwordless-phone#user-registration-and-management-of-microsoft-authenticator) for more information.
