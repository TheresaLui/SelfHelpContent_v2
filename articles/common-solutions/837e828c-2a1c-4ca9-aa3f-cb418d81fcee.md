It looks like you're having issues with monitoring device health during an update. Most users are able to resolve their device health monitoring issue using the steps below.

## **Recommended Steps**

1. Verify that the manufacturer and model properties of a targeted device have not been changed after connecting the device to IoT Hub.
2. Check the [Device Update troubleshooting guide](https://) to see if your issue is addressed. If you are still encountering issues, please create a new support request.
