# Free Trial Subscription Expired

If your free trial subscription has expired, Microsoft does not remove peering connection from devices and your peering sessions would not go down.

## **Recommended Steps**

1. If you have created a new subscription and want to use that for managing resource, please send email to [Peering Service](mailto:peeringservice@microsoft.com) with subject "Remove Free trial subscription from resource **Subscription Id** **Resource Group Name** **Peering Name**
2. The process may take up to three days to complete. Once the resources is disassociate from expired subscription, you can map it to the new subscription
