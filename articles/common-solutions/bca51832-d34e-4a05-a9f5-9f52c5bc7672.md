# Configure SSL or HTTPS 

To enable the HTTPS protocol for securely delivering content on a Front Door custom domain, you must use an SSL certificate. You can choose to use a certificate that is managed by Azure Front Door Service, or import your own.

## **Recommended Documents**

* [How to configure HTTPS on a Front Door custom domain onboarded on Front Door](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/frontdoor/front-door-custom-domain-https?tabs=option-1-default-enable-https-with-an-afd-managed-certificate)
* [Onboard a custom domain with HTTPS](https://azure.microsoft.com/resources/templates/101-front-door-custom-domain/)
* [Certificate authorities](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/frontdoor/front-door-troubleshoot-allowed-ca) for enabling custom HTTPS on Azure Front Door Service<br>
