# Configure SSL or HTTPS 

To enable the HTTPS protocol for securely delivering content on a Front Door custom domain, you must use an SSL certificate. You can choose to use a certificate that is managed by Azure Front Door service, or import your own.

## **Recommended Documents**

* [How to configure HTTPS on a Front Door custom domain onboarded on Front Door](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/frontdoor/standard-premium/how-to-configure-https-custom-domain)

* [Certificate authorities](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/frontdoor/standard-premium/troubleshoot-allowed-certificate-authority) for enabling custom HTTPS on Azure Front Door Service<br>
