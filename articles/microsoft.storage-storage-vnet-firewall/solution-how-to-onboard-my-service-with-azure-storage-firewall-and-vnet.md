# How to onboard my service with Azure Storage Firewall and VNET

The following section is designed to provide guidance on how to Onboard an Application/Service with Azure Storage Firewall and VNETs as a [Trusted Service](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/storage/common/storage-network-security#trusted-microsoft-services).

## Guidance

1. Check if there is already guidance for the the Application or Service in question at the previous section.
   * If there is no guidance and/or on-boarding is needed, please continue.
2. Engage Support team responsible of the involved Service or Application.
3. The appropriate support team shall create an escalation with their Application/Service Product Group(PG).
4. The component PG shall review and Onboard their Service/Application with Storage Firewall and VNETs. Further information below:
   * [**VNet Integration for Azure Services Website**](https://aka.ms/vnetonboarding)
   * [**Onboarding FAQ**](https://microsoft.sharepoint.com/teams/vnet/SitePages/FAQs.aspx)
   * Sign up for an in-person time: [https://aka.ms/VNetIntegrationOfficeHours](https://aka.ms/VNetIntegrationOfficeHours)
   * Contact: ***vnetonboarding@microsoft.com***
