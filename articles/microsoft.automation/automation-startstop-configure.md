# Azure Automation - Configuring Start-Stop Solution
This article explains the various ways to configure the [Start/Stop VM Solution](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/automation/automation-solution-vm-management)

## **Recommended Steps**

**Modify start/stop times**

* Review the ["Modify the startup and shutdown schedules document](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/automation/automation-solution-vm-management#modify-the-startup-and-shutdown-schedules)

**Add a VM**

* Review the ["Add a VM" document](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/automation/automation-solution-vm-management#add-a-vm)

**Exclude a VM**

* Review the ["Remove a VM" document](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/automation/automation-solution-vm-management#exclude-a-vm)

**Configure email notifications**

* Review the ["Configure email notifications" document](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/automation/automation-solution-vm-management#configure-email-notifications)

**Remove the Start/Stop solution**

* Review the ["Remove the solution" document](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/automation/automation-solution-vm-management#remove-the-solution)
