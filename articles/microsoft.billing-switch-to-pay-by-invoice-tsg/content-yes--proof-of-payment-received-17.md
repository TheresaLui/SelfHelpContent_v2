# Proof of payment received

Once you have Proof of Payment from the customer, open the a collaboration task with GFS Cash Apps team (Queue: GCS Incoming - Cash Apps) to trace and apply the payment, indicating the customer intends to move to Invoice PI. Ensure to fill the following template:

1.	Company name:
2.	Order numbers:
3.	Invoice numbers:
4.	Note: If one payment covers multiple invoices, include the payment amount for each invoice.
5.	Payment amounts:
6.	Currency type:
7.	Payment date:
8.	Reason for inquiry:
9.	Payment details (Choose one of the following):
10.	If paid by check, include the following:
11.	Check number:
12.	Address the check was sent to:
13.	Last four digits of the Microsoft Bank account number:
14.	If paid by wire or automated clearinghouse (ACH), include the following:
15.	Last four digits of the Microsoft Bank account number the check was sent to:
16.	Remittance details clearly indicating the specific invoices that the payment is to be applied to.
17.	These details include:
18.	Release date of the payment from the bank:
19.	Order ID (Located on the bottom of the first page of the invoice):
Note: If paid by wire or ACH, the payment amount must be shown on the bank issued remittances. This information is necessary to ensure payments are applied accurately as customers may have multiple accounts with similar company names.

Best Practice: Whenever possible, attach the bank remittances to the task.
