# Account admin/contact email of customer profile does not match company tenant details in CMAT/CST

Seek approval from a Global Administrator of the Tenant to validate if the Subscription belongs to the organization, by sending an email to the Global admin of the tenant. You can get the Global Administrator (Company Administrator) email from CMAT/CST when you search with customer's company domain or tenant ID

Additionally, obtain legal document to validate the company details. 
For all businesses except for individuals, we require the below documents:

1.	A Certificate of Incorporation signed by the company?s legal representatives.
2.	Any Government issued documents having the company name and address. (i.e., Tax Certification) Example: Employer Identification Number (EIN) Letter Confirmation from Internal Revenue Service (IRS)
3.	Company Registration form signed and issued by the government.

For Government Agencies, below are example documents that can be submitted:

1.	Tax Exemption Certificate
2.	VAT Return
3.	Federal Record or Registration
4.	Tax Identification Number (TIN) Record issued by Internal Revenue Department
5.	Budget Report
6.	Orders of the Court
