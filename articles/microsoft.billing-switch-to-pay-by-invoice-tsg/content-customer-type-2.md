# Customer Type

Inform the customer of the following:

1. It is not possible to return to credit card payment after changing to Invoice Payment.
2. Invoice Payment is available only for business accounts, not personal accounts.
3. All Billing information must be up to date before changing to Invoice Payment.
4. With Invoice Payment, Wire Transfers will be manually performed every month and bank fees might apply. 
5. We will review the account to determine if it is eligible for Invoice Payment, but not all requests are approved.
6. Pay all outstanding charges before switching to invoice pay. 
7. Currently, invoice payment isn't supported for Global Azure in China.
