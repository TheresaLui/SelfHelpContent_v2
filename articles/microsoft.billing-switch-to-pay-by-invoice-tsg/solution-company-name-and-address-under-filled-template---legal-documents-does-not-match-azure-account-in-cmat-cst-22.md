# Company Name and Address under filled template / legal documents does not match Azure Account in CMAT/CST

<!--issueDescription-->

Please visit the link: [Change azure account profile](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/cost-management-billing/manage/change-azure-account-profile) for instruction on how to update your Company Details on Azure Account.

Also you may have one of the Global Admins of the tenant follow the steps below to update the profile on a tenant level :

1. Open the following link and login using Global Administrator credentials: [Company profile](https://admin.microsoft.com/Adminportal/#/companyprofile)
2. Under Organization profile, click Edit and ensure all the fields are updated.
