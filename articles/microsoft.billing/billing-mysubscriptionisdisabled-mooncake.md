# My subscription is disabled

## **Recommended Steps**

* [Find your Account Admin](data-blade:Microsoft_Azure_Billing.SubscriptionPropertiesBlade)
* Your Account Admin may have canceled the subscription. If it's a Pay-As-You-Go subscription, the Account Admin can reactivate it in the Account Center. [Reactivate a canceled subscription](https://docs.azure.cn/billing/billing-subscription-become-disable#the-subscription-was-canceled-by-the-account-administrator).
