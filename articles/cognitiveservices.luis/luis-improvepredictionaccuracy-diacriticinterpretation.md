# Diacritic interpretation

### Getting Started

* Diacritics are marks or signs within text in some foreign languages.

* This affects the training of LUIS models. [LUIS allows the option to normalize diacritics](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/cognitive-services/luis/how-to-application-settings-portal#change-version-setting) which allows you to negate the effect of diacritics if you wish.

* You will find this option in the bottom of the **Settings** page under the **Manage** section in the top bar.

## **Recommended Steps**

* Learn more about [Diacritics](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/cognitive-services/luis/luis-concept-utterance#diacritics).

* Learn about [utterance normalization](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/cognitive-services/luis/luis-concept-utterance#utterance-normalization).

* Learn what are [good utterances for your LUIS app](https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/cognitive-services/luis/luis-concept-utterance).
