    # Check if customer issue applies to this Troubleshooting Guide

    This TSG has been designed to assist you with customer's that are configuring their VPN devices. If your customer is having a different issue you may need to find another TSG.

# How to verify the customer issue
1. Check the customer verbatim to ensure they are setting up a VPN Site-to-Site connection
2. Talk to the customer to verify what they need assistance with
3. Make sure the Support Topic and Product map to VPN and S2S connectivity/setup
