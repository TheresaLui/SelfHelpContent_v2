# I am receiving an exception

## Why am I getting a timeout exception?
This exception usually indicates that a user-initiated operation is taking longer than the operation timeout. Additional info can be found here: <https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/documentation/articles/service-bus-messaging-exceptions/>

## Why am I getting a quota exceeded exception?
QuotaExceededException indicates that a quota for a specific entity has been exceeded. Additional info on this exception can be found here: <https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/documentation/articles/service-bus-messaging-exceptions/>

## Are you experiencing other types of Exceptions?
You can find some common exceptions and solutions here: <https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/documentation/articles/service-bus-messaging-exceptions/>
