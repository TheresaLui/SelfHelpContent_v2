# Errors and Exceptions/400 Bad Request for Pricing Tier Update

## **Recommended steps**
* This may happen when you are trying to downgrade and the number of active devices exceeds the allowed limit for the tier you want to downgrade to.
* Delete devices to meet the tier limits before downgrading.
