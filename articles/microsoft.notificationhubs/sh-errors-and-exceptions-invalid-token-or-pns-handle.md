# Errors and Exceptions/Invalid token or PNS handle

## **Recommended steps**
* Verify the device token obtained from Platform Notification Systems on your client.<br>
* [Verify registrations](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkID=824679&clcid=0x409) of all devices.<br>
